Controls

  • Click on the map to add a point.
    • CTRL-click on a point to add a point at its exact location (useful for closing loops).
  • Right click to remove the selected point.
  • Click on a point to select it.
  • Drag a point to move it.
    • Press and hold CTRL while dragging a point to snap it to another point's location.
